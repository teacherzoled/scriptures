Welcome! Who is God?

Hello, everyone! Today, we're going on an exciting journey to understand God better. We're going to learn about His attributes. That's a big word, but it just means we're looking at His character—who God is and what He is like.

These attributes are like superpowers that belong only to God. They show us how powerful, loving, and perfect He is. Learning about them will help us appreciate Him even more!

Let's Learn God's Amazing Attributes

Let's explore ten of God's attributes. As we learn each one, think about how amazing our God truly is!

Omniscient

All-Knowing

God knows everything, and His knowledge is complete. He knows the past, the present, and the future. He even knows our thoughts and the number of hairs on our head!

"Great is our Lord and mighty in power; his understanding has no limit." (Psalm 147:5)

🙏 Reflection: God knows everything about me and still loves me perfectly.

Omnipotent

All-Powerful

God is able to do everything that He chooses. Nothing is too hard for Him! He created the entire universe with just His words. He has unlimited power.

"I know that you can do all things; no purpose of yours can be thwarted." (Job 42:2)

🙏 Reflection: The all-powerful God is on my side and can help me with any problem.

Omnipresent

Present Everywhere

God is present in all places at all times. You can't go anywhere where God is not. He is with you at home, at school, and wherever you go. You are never alone!

"Where can I go from your Spirit? Where can I flee from your presence? If I go up to the heavens, you are there; if I make my bed in the depths, you are there." (Psalm 139:7-8)

🙏 Reflection: I can talk to God anytime, anywhere, because He is always with me.

Immutable

Unchanging

God is absolutely unchanging. His love, His promises, and His character are the same yesterday, today, and forever. We can always depend on Him because He never changes.

"I the Lord do not change. So you, the descendants of Jacob, are not destroyed." (Malachi 3:6)

🙏 Reflection: In a world that is always changing, I can trust in my unchanging God.

Holy

Perfect and Pure

God is perfectly pure, without any sin or evil. His holiness is so bright and powerful that He cannot even look at sin. He is set apart from everything else.

"Holy, holy, holy is the Lord Almighty; the whole earth is full of his glory." (Isaiah 6:3)

🙏 Reflection: Because God is holy, I want to live a life that pleases Him.

Righteous

Always Right and Just

Because God is holy, everything He does is right and fair. He is the perfect judge. His holiness is expressed through His righteousness when He deals with people.

"The Lord is righteous in all his ways and faithful in all he does." (Psalm 145:17)

🙏 Reflection: I can trust God to be fair and just in every situation.

Sovereign

The Supreme Ruler

God is the king of the universe, and He rules over His creation. He is in complete control and is free to do what He knows is best for us. Even when things seem confusing, God has a plan.

"In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth." (Genesis 1:1)

🙏 Reflection: I can have peace knowing that God is in control of my life and the world.

Love

He IS Love

This is one of the most wonderful attributes! God doesn't just *have* love; He *is* love. His love is shown through His mercy and grace. He wants to have a personal relationship with each of us.

"Whoever does not love does not know God, because God is love." (1 John 4:8)

🙏 Reflection: God loves me unconditionally, no matter what I do.

Merciful

Full of Mercy

Mercy means God doesn't give us the punishment we deserve for our sins. Instead of punishment, He offers us forgiveness and salvation through Jesus. The story of the Prodigal Son in Luke 15 is a beautiful picture of God's mercy.

"But because of his great love for us, God, who is rich in mercy, made us alive with Christ even when we were dead in transgressions—it is by grace you have been saved." (Ephesians 2:4-5)

🙏 Reflection: I am so thankful for God's mercy that forgives my mistakes.

Trinity

Three-in-One

This can be tricky, but it's so important! God is one, but He exists as three distinct persons: God the Father, God the Son (Jesus), and God the Holy Spirit. We see all three at Jesus' baptism in Matthew 3!

"Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit." (Matthew 28:19)

🙏 Reflection: The Father, Son, and Holy Spirit all work together and love me.

You are Loved by an Awesome God!

Great job today!

We've learned about some of God's amazing attributes: He is Omniscient (all-knowing), Omnipotent (all-powerful), Omnipresent (everywhere), Immutable (unchanging), Holy, Righteous, Sovereign, Love, Merciful, and a Trinity.

These aren't just facts to memorize; they show us the character of the one true God. They help us understand His power, His love, and His care for us.

This week, remember one of these attributes when you pray. If you're scared, remember He is Omnipotent. If you feel alone, remember He is Omnipresent. He is an awesome God!
